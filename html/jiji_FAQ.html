Frequently Asked Questions

Where do your coffee beans come from?

All of our beans are sourced from growers in California and are shipped whole to be ground in store, so your coffee has that fresh, rich taste we are known for, every time.

Can I volunteer in the store and for events?

Yes! We welcome volunteers and would love to review your application. Please send an application to jijis.beans@gmail.com. We will get back to you within 3 days if you are accepted.

How long will it take for my adoption application to go through?

It depends on which shelter the cat is being adopted through. Most applications do not take longer than a week to go through, so expect a response within that time frame.

Can I bring my cat to visit with your feline residents?

For the safety of our cats, we ask that you do not bring in any outside animals. Thank you!

What medical care have the cats received?

Our residents are up to date with all required vaccinations, and all of our adult residents (1 year and older) have been spayed or neutered. Additionally, we have them regularly tested for parasites, and all of them have been treated for fleas and ticks.
