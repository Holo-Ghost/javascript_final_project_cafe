What are Paw Points?

Paw Points are a rewards system used to provide our members with special deals. With just an email, name, and phone number, members can:

  • Earn 25 Paw Points with every $5 spent
  • Receive special offers on drinks and food through email or phone
  • Get the latest updates on our feline residents
  • Be the first to know about special events and fundraisers
